📘 Digital Notes Organizer

Stay Tuned!

Digital Notes Organizer is an advanced web-based application that helps users store, organize, and retrieve notes with ease. Designed for students, professionals, and creators who want a clean and efficient space to manage all their ideas digitally.

🔧 Project Highlights:

  • 📝 Easy note creation with formatting support
  • 🔍 Real-time search & tagging functionality
  • 📁 Organized folders & categories
  • 🌐 Sync across multiple devices (planned)
  • 🔐 Secure login & private notes lock
  • 🎨 Dark mode + custom color themes
  • 📊 Notes summary insights & stats
